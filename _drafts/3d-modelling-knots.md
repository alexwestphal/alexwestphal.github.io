## Linear Bézier Curve

$$ B_{linear}(t) = (1-t)P_0 + tP_1, t \in [0,1]$$

## Quadratic Bézier Curve

$$ B_{quadratic}(t) = (1-t)^2P_0 + 2(1-t)tP_1 + t^2P_2 , t \in [0,1] $$



## Cubic Bézier Curve

$$ B_{cubic}(t) = (1-t)^3P_0 + 3(1-t)^2tP_1 + 3(1-t)t^2P_2 + t^3P_3 , t \in [0,1]$$
